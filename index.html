Quick & Easy Microwave Brownie

You can make a delicious brownie from scratch in less than 5 minutes, using 5 ingredients (plus a mug, a microwave, and a measuring spoon).

Here’s How

In a microwave-safe mug, combine:

  • 2 Tbsp flour
  • 2 Tbsp sugar
  • 1 Tbsp cocoa
  • 1 Tbsp water
  • 1 Tbsp butter

Mix thoroughly, then microwave for 1 minute. Wasn’t that easy?
